Cup Matcher - A 3D Printable Logic Game

How to Play

Setup

The game can be played in multiple ways, depending on the number of players and sets available:

  • Single Set Play: Use one set of cups to have one player as the "arranger" and the other as the "guesser."
  • Dual Set Competition: With two sets, both players take turns being the arranger and guesser, competing to see who can guess the correct order in the fewest rounds.
  • Multi-Set Challenge: For more than two players or to increase the difficulty, print more than four sets. One player guesses the order of all sets simultaneously, adding a new level of challenge and strategy.

Objective

The arranger secretly arranges their set of cups in a specific order under the box. The guesser must deduce the exact order of the cups by arranging their own set of cups.

Gameplay

  • The guesser arranges their cups in a guessed order.
  • The arranger then gives feedback, indicating how many cups are in the correct spot (without specifying which cups or positions).
  • The guesser uses this feedback to adjust their arrangement in the next round.
  • This process repeats until the guesser has correctly matched the order of all the cups.

Scoring

Each round, the guesser is scored based on how many cups are correctly positioned. The game can continue with the same or different players taking on the roles of arranger and guesser.

Winning

The game ends when the guesser successfully matches all the cups in the correct order. The player with the fewest rounds taken to guess correctly wins!
